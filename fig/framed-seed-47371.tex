\quoteshape
You come across some bandits attacking a merchant.
 
The bandits are threatening the merchant.
 
What do you do?

\begin{enumerate}[itemsep=0pt,topsep=4pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
\item You talk the bandits down  \\
(You are missing skill: negotiation). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{2}$\rightarrow$  You talk with the bandits, but the bandits refuse to back down.  The bandits have started to threaten you. \\[-0.5em]
\item You transform the bandits into a chicken  \\
(You have skill: sorcery. You have your ancient grimoire). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{11}$\rightarrow$ You cast a spell on the bandits, and the bandits are cursed with the form of a chicken. The bandits are no longer threatening the merchant, they have been cursed and transformed into a chicken, and they are now unintelligent. \\[-0.5em]
\item You travel onwards  \\
(no relevant skills). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{0}$\rightarrow$ You continue your journey. You have traveled to a new place.
\end{enumerate}
}
