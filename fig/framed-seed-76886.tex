\slshape
 As you continue your travels, an ogre approaches you menacingly, gnashing its teeth. 
 
She is threatening you.
 
What do you do?

\begin{enumerate}[itemsep=0pt,topsep=4pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
\item You transform the ogre into a chicken  \\
(You have skill: sorcery. You have your ancient grimoire). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{7}$\rightarrow$  You cast a spell on the ogre, and the ogre is cursed with the form of a chicken.  The ogre is no longer threatening you, and she has been transformed into a chicken. \\[-0.5em]
\item You try to flee from the ogre  \\
(You have skill: acrobatics. She is missing skill: acrobatics). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{1}$\rightarrow$ You flee from her and escape. The ogre is no longer threatening you. \\[-0.5em]
\item You attack the ogre  \\
(You have skill: fighting. She has skill: fighting. You have your spear. She has no tool for fighting). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{2}$\rightarrow$ You attack the ogre. She strikes at you, but then you avoid the ogre's attack, then you thrust at the ogre. She is defeated. The ogre is not threatening you any more, and she is injured now.
\end{enumerate}
}
