\quoteshape
 As you journey onwards, an ogre menacingly approaches you, waving its arms. 
 
She is threatening you.
 
What do you do?

\begin{enumerate}[itemsep=0pt,topsep=4pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
\item You transform the ogre into a chicken  \\
(You are missing skill: sorcery). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{0}$\rightarrow$  You try to cast a spell on the ogre, but nothing happens.  The situation remains the same. \\[-0.5em]
\item You try to flee from the ogre  \\
(The ogre is missing skill: acrobatics. You have skill: acrobatics). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{12}$\rightarrow$ You flee from the ogre and escape. The ogre is not threatening you any more. \\[-0.5em]
\item You attempt to pacify the ogre with music  \\
(You are missing skill: musician). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{0}$\rightarrow$ You try to pacify the ogre, but the ogre becomes enraged. You are injured now, and the ogre has started to threaten you.
\end{enumerate}
}
