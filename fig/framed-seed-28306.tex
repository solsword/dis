 As you continue your journey, an ogre approaches you, stomping its feet. 
 
She is threatening you.
 
What do you do?

\begin{enumerate}[itemsep=0pt,topsep=4pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
\item You attempt to pacify the ogre with music  \\
(You have skill: musician. You have no tool for music). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{1}$\rightarrow$  You weave a soothing melody, and the ogre becomes calm.  The ogre is no longer threatening you. \\[-0.5em]
\item You try to flee from the ogre  \\
(The ogre has skill: wilderness lore. You are missing skill: wilderness lore). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{1}$\rightarrow$ You flee from the ogre and escape. The ogre is no longer threatening you. \\[-0.5em]
\item You attack the ogre  \\
(The ogre has skill: fighting. You have skill: fighting. She has no tool for fighting. You have your spear). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{10}$\rightarrow$ You attack the ogre. You strike at the ogre and then the ogre fights back. Finally you and the ogre withdraw from combat, exhausted. You are now injured.
\end{enumerate}
}
