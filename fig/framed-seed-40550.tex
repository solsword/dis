\slshape
You come across some bandits attacking a merchant.
 
The bandits are threatening the merchant.
 
What do you do?

\begin{enumerate}[itemsep=0pt,topsep=4pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
\item You attack the bandits  \\
(The bandits have skill: fighting. You have skill: fighting. They have no tool for fighting. You have your spear). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{4}$\rightarrow$  You attack the bandits. You dodge the bandits' attack but then the bandits dodge your attack and the bandits fight back. Suddenly the bandits rally and fiercely strike back at you, and you are defeated.  You have been killed. \\[-0.5em]
\item You transform the bandits into a chicken  \\
(You have skill: sorcery. You have your ancient grimoire). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{9}$\rightarrow$ You try to cast a spell on the bandits, but nothing happens. The situation remains the same. \\[-0.5em]
\item You offer the bandits your perfume  \\
(You have skill: negotiation). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{1}$\rightarrow$ You bargain with the bandits, but can not seem to reach a deal. The situation remains the same.
\end{enumerate}
}
