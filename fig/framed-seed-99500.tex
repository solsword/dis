\slshape
 As you journey onwards, an ogre approaches you slowly, gnashing its teeth. 
 
She is threatening you.
 
What do you do?

\begin{enumerate}[itemsep=0pt,topsep=4pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
\item You attack the ogre  \\
(You are missing skill: fighting. She has skill: fighting. She has no tool for fighting). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{4}$\rightarrow$  You attack her. You strike at her but she thrusts at you and then you are hit by the ogre's attack. Suddenly she rallies and fiercely strikes back at you, and you are defeated.  The ogre is no longer threatening you, and she is now injured. \\[-0.5em]
\item You attempt to pacify the ogre with music  \\
(You are missing skill: musician). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{7}$\rightarrow$ You weave a soothing melody, and the ogre becomes calm. The ogre is not threatening you any more. \\[-0.5em]
\item You transform the ogre into a chicken  \\
(You are missing skill: sorcery). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{0}$\rightarrow$ You cast a spell on the ogre, and the ogre is cursed with the form of a chicken. The ogre is no longer threatening you, and she has been transformed into a chicken.
\end{enumerate}
}
