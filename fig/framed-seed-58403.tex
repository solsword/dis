 As you travel onwards, an ogre menacingly approaches you, gnashing its teeth. 
 
She is threatening you.
 
What do you do?

\begin{enumerate}[itemsep=0pt,topsep=4pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
\item You transform the ogre into a chicken  \\
(You are missing skill: sorcery). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{3}$\rightarrow$  You try to cast a spell on the ogre, but nothing happens.  The situation remains the same. \\[-0.5em]
\item You attack the ogre  \\
(The ogre has skill: fighting. You are missing skill: fighting. She has no tool for fighting). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{1}$\rightarrow$ You attack her. You strike at her but then she avoids your attack, and you are hit by the ogre's attack. Suddenly she rallies and fiercely strikes back at you, and you are defeated. You become injured. \\[-0.5em]
\item You try to flee from the ogre  \\
(The ogre has skill: wilderness lore. You are missing skill: wilderness lore). \\[0.3em]
\choicecount{8}$\rightarrow$ You flee from the ogre but can not escape. You are now injured.
\end{enumerate}
